# Mumsy's Crumble

.

## Ingredients

- Cooking apples or other fruit 400 g
- Plain flour 100 g
- Hard Margerine or butter 50 g
- Sugar (white or brown) 50 g
- Optional topping, oats, brown sugar, muesli, nuts, grated orange peel

## Method

1. Nake up the crumble by rubbing the fat into the flour until it looks like crumbs, and then add the sugar and mix.
2. Grease a dish, cover the bottom of the dish with the fruit, put crumble mixture on top of the fruit, then sprinkle oats and brown sugar on top.
3. Cook at 160-180 C for about 40 to 45 minutes.

## Notes

1. When you cook the fruit don't add water because it will make the crumble go soggy.
2. I cook the apple just a bit, say, 5 minutes plus?
3. Rhubard and plum I don't cook as it cooks with the crumble.
4. Options - for toppings muesli and or nuts, hazelnuts, almonds or walnuts, also the nuts can be ground into the crumble mixture, if liked, and grated orange peel in the mixture is good.
