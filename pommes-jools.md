# Crispy Layered Potatoes

Serves 8 as a side

## Ingredients

- Maris Piper potatoes 1.5 kg
- Fresh rosemary 10 g
- Fresh thyme 10 g
- Olive oil
- Salt and pepper

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 180 C.
2. Peel the potatoes and slice as finely as you can, ideally 2 mm thick.
3. Pick and chop the rosemary and thyme.
4. Drizzle with 2 tbsp of olive oil and toss it all together with a pinch of sea salt and black pepper.
5. Line a 24 cm non-stick ovenproof frying pan with greaseproof paper, then rub with olive oil.
6. Layer up the potatoes,pressing down well to compact, then cover with tin foil and bake for 1.5 h.
7. Remove from the oven and leave to cool.
8. Now evenly press down on the foil and refrigerate overnight.
9. Next day preheat oven to 220 C.
10. Remove the foil and turn the potatoes out on to a board and remove the paper.
11. Cut the potatoes into 3 cm slices, turning each slice onto its side to expose the layers.
12. Rub lots of butter into the pan.
13. Put the potatoes back in the pan on their sides like a jigsaw, compacting really well.
14. Cook in the bottom of the oven for 30 min or until golden on the bottom.
15. Turn out onto serving plate.
