# Lemon Posset

Serve some people.

## Ingredients

- Cream 1 pt
- Sugar 6 oz
- Lemons 2
- Macaroons (?)

## Method

1. Pare the peel from the lemons very thinly and boil with the cream.

1. Let it cool and when luke warm add the lemon juice sweetened with sugar.

1. Pour mixture over macarooms placed in a glass dish.
