# Lemon Posset

Serve some people.

## Ingredients

- Single cream 600 ml
- Caster sugar 180 g
- Lemons 2
- Amaretti biscuits

## Method

1. Pare the peel from the lemons very thinly (or just zest) and boil with the cream.

1. Let it cool and when luke warm add the lemon juice and sugar.

1. Pour mixture over biscuits placed in a glass dish.
